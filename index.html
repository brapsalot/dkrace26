FAQ

How does Take Control work?

Choose an effect from the Donate tab, copy the generated message, and include it in your Streamlabs donation. The server reads your donation message and activates the effect!

  • $1+ — Control 1 streamer's game for 30 seconds
  • $3+ — Control ALL streamers at once for 30 seconds
  • $5+ — Trigger the DK Rap — locks all streamers for the full song!

What happens when I take control?

Once your donation is confirmed, a virtual SNES gamepad appears on screen. You have 30 seconds to play the streamer's game using your keyboard or by clicking the on-screen buttons. Your inputs are sent directly to the streamer's emulator in real time — you are literally playing their game for them!

Stream Overlay Buttons

Hover over any stream to reveal these buttons:

  • 🎮 Control — Opens the Donate tab with this streamer pre-selected
  • CC — Opens the Crowd Control effects page in a popup
  • ⛶ Focus — Enlarges this stream (click again to restore grid)
  • ✕ Hide — Hides this stream from the grid

Draw Mode ✎

Click the pencil icon in the top-right of the stream grid to toggle draw mode. While active you can draw on top of the streams — your strokes are visible to all viewers and fade away after a few seconds. Use the colour picker and brush size slider to customise.

DK Raps Counter

The DK Raps button in the top bar shows how many DK Rap effects have been triggered. Click it to jump straight to the DK Rap donation flow.
